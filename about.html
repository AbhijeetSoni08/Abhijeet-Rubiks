About Us

Welcome to Rubik's, the vibrant cultural club of Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology! Rubik's is a hub of creativity, expression, and talent, where students come together to celebrate art, music, dance, drama, literature, and much more. Our club serves as a platform for nurturing and showcasing the diverse skills of our members while fostering a sense of community and collaboration. Whether it's organizing exhilarating events, hosting captivating workshops, or participating in inter-college competitions, Rubik's thrives on energy and passion. At Rubik's, we believe in the power of culture to inspire, unite, and transform. Join us to be part of an ever-evolving journey of creativity and celebration!
